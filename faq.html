Our Refund Policy

If you are not satisfied with the services that you have purchased from us, you can get your money back no questions asked. You are eligible for a 50% reimbursement within 14 calendar days of your service End Date. After the 14-day period you will no longer be eligible and won’t be able to receive a refund.
